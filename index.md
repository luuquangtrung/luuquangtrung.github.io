I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Avignon and the University of Toulouse. 

Prior to that, I was a Ph.D candidate at CentraleSupélec - Université Paris-Saclay, and was also a research engineer at Nokia Bell Labs, France.

During the PhD I did research on the dynamic control and optimization of wireless virtual networks, especially in designing efficient resource provisioning algorithms for network slicing. 

During the postdoc, I focus on the management and resource allocation for coflows in datacenters.
